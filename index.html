5th - 9th February 2018: Vanbrugh Bowl

‘What matters most?’ As students we often wonder about purpose, significance and meaning. Join us as we explore different life pursuits. We’d love to hear about what matters most to you.

Look forward to free lunches with big questions, an afternoon coffeehouse and evening events with life changing stories.
