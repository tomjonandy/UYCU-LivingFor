‘What matters most?’ As students we often wonder our purpose in life, wondering what comes next and searching for a plan for our lives. We all want our life to have meaning and be worth while but what makes it so? When will we finally be satisfied that we are heading on the right track?

Join us as we explore the power of God to change our lives and become the foundation of our lives, giving us meaning. Free lunches with big questions, a coffeehouse and evening events with life changing stories and more, scroll down for more information...

THE VENUE

The Living For Marquee will be the venue for lunches, coffeehouse and evenings. We want you to feel welcome there, even if you’re just dying for a cuppa, don’t hesitate to drop in and make yourself at home. It is located on Vanbrugh Bowl, just in front of the Music Department.

LUNCHES

A chance to explore the Big Questions people have about what matters in their life and how Christianity does or does not fit into this. Accompanied by a free lunch, there is a chance afterwards to have more conversation and ask further questions.

12pm - 1pm, in the Living For Marquee

Monday 12:00

Science: Can anyone take the Bible seriously?

The basis of the Christian faith, in a world of modern technology and science, can this text still be reliable and have a place in the world?

Tuesday 12:00

Freedom: Won’t following Jesus cost me my Freedom?

Student life can be very liberating, the chance to live as we please. Can Christianity really provide the same sense of freedom or is it just about following rules?

Wednesday 12:00

Pleasure: Why would a good God allow so much suffering?

As we turn on the TV or look at the world around us, we see so much pain and loss. How can a God with unconditional love allow his creation to go through so much sorrow?

Thursday 12:00

Purpose: Does anything I do really matter?

Does anything i do really matter? Surely life is more than just uni work, sleeping and clubbing? How can we find a purpose to our life and where does God fit into this?

Friday 12:00

Eternity: Did Jesus rise from the Dead?

Over 2000 years ago, this miracle has seemingly changed the world and the lives of many. Is it merely an unfounded miracle or is there any evidence to suggest its truth?

EVENINGS

Our heated marquee will be a relaxed and welcoming place each evening, to take a break from the stresses of uni and explore more about what matters in our lives. It will be home to student dramas, entertainment and a range of testimonies from people who have been changed through living for Jesus. Each evening will include a small talk centred around the Bible, looking at how God can provide meaning to all areas in our lives.

7:30pm - 9:30pm, in the Living For Marquee

Monday 19:30

Living For Answers

Join us as we explore how science and Christianity can work together to provide answers in life and are not opposing fields.

Featuring: Student dramas and a testimony.

Tuesday 19:30

Living For Freedom

We will explore how our desire to be free and make our own choices can lead us to trouble. Join us to hear how one man’s choice to follow Jesus liberated rather than restricted him.

Featuring: an interview with ex Yorkshire prisoner, Shane Taylor.

Wednesday 19:30

Living for Happiness

with so much pain and suffering in the world, happiness can seem a hard thing to achieve. Join us as we explore how God can bring joy into our lives even in the most difficult of circumstances.

Featuring: student dramas and a student testimony.

Thursday 19:30

Living For Success

We all want to feel as though we have made it in life and be considered successful. Join us as we hear how success is not always all it promises and how our purpose in life can be found through the love of Jesus.

Featuring: an interview with ex Gladiator Warren Thurman.

Friday 19:30

Living For Eternity

What happens if we do decide to follow Jesus and decide that we want to life for God? Join us for our final evening as we look at the resurrection of Jesus and how this matters to us today.

Featuring: student dramas and a testimony.

COFFEEHOUSE

Whether you want a place to study, a study break or a chance to ponder over your questions with a cup of hot chocolate, the Living For Coffeehouse is here for you! Every afternoon from 2pm - 4pm, we are proud to serve a range of hot and cold drinks including lattes, cappuccinos and some scrumptious cakes! All for free, of course.

INTERNATIONAL

On Tuesday, come along to the Living For marquee from 5-6:30pm to meet with other International students. There will be a short talk with a meal provided! Everybody welcome!
